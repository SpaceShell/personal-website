About Home for Healthy Habits

Home for Healthy Habits in a non-profit organization that seeks to teach youth healthy eating habits. We take the time to teach children aged 6-18 good sources of food vs bad sources of food. In every community we serve we build community gardens with our students, and grow healthy fruits and vegetables, for the community to indulge in at no cost. We also use the fruits and vegetables grown in our local garden to teach students healthy recipes during our weekly cooking courses.
